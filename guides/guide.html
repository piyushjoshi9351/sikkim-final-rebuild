Visitor Guide

Your complete companion for exploring the sacred monasteries of Sikkim.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: March to June & September to November.
  • Carry comfortable walking shoes for monastery tours.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Photography may be restricted inside certain temples.
  • Altitude can affect health; keep hydrated and rest often.

Nearby Facilities

Hotels, restaurants, and emergency services are available near major monasteries.

Things to Know

Monasteries are peaceful places. Maintain silence, avoid littering, and dress modestly. Respect monks and local residents.
